abstract: >-
  Invited speaker presentation at the XIII. Turkish Accounting Expertise Congress held on December 5-7, 2025 in Gaziantep, Turkey. The congress theme was "The Future and Boundaries of Accounting: From Artificial Intelligence to Space Accounting" (Muhasebenin Geleceği ve Sınırları: Yapay Zekadan Uzay Muhasebesine). The presentation titled "From Data to Value | Artificial Intelligence as a Lever in Financial Systems" explored how artificial intelligence technologies are transforming financial systems, accounting practices, and decision-making processes in the modern economy. The talk addressed AI-driven innovations in financial data analysis, automated accounting systems, and the emerging role of intelligent systems in shaping the future of the accounting profession.
